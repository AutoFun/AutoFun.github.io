🖊Energy transmission loss

If P is in MW and l *in km then *k is about 1.5
V is the line-to-line voltage; more as we progress.

损耗跟距离和k有关
$$
\begin{align}
& V \approx k \sqrt{P l} \quad *
\\
&R = k_{1} l
\\
&P_{\mathrm{loss}}=\frac{P^{2}}{V^{2}} k_{1} l
\end{align}
$$
If losses are to be kept at a fraction k2 of the transmitted power,
$$
P_{loss}=Pk_{2} \quad \quad \quad \frac{P^{2}}{V^{2}} k_{1} l=k_{2} P\quad \quad \quad \frac{P l k_{1}}{k_{2}}=V^{2} \quad \quad \quad k=\sqrt{\frac{k_{1}}{k_{2}}}
\\
V \propto \sqrt{P l}
$$
